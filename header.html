The Khudayarkhan Museum

Kokan

Khudoyorkhan Ordasy - an architectural monument in Kokand (second half of the 19th century); It was built by Kokand khan Khudoyorkhan. Designed and directed by architect and engineer Mir Ubaydulla; Mulla Suyarqul, Solikhoja and Fozilkhoja from Bukhara took part in the competition. Khudoyorkhan Ordas has a rectangular shape (68x143 m) and is built on an artificial hill with a high brick foundation.
